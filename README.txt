Want to increase revenue of your e-commerce store ?

WooCommerce Cross-sell Popup stimulate the purchase of customers with the products cross sell popup. Increase revenue as well as profitability for your e-commerce stores.

WooCommerce Cross-sell Popup allow to show combo, packs or product group with lower price that customer can buy when he add new product to the cart to sell more and increase e-commerce revenues.

== Important Notice ==
Plugin works based on WooCommerce plugin. Tested on WooCommerce version 3.5 or higher

== Features ==

- Enable or Disable cross-sells products
- Enable or Disable cross-sells products on mobile

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the download package
2. Upload `dt-woocommerce-cross-sell-popup.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
